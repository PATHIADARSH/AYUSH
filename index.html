AYUSH HEALTH CARE CHATBOT

AYUSH is built on Azure, a cloud computing platform by Microsoft that offers a wide range of services to support the development and deployment of AI applications. With Azure's powerful and secure infrastructure, AYUSH can store and process large amounts of data, deliver fast and reliable responses, and scale seamlessly to meet the growing demands of users. AYUSH leverages Azure's cognitive services, such as natural language processing and machine learning, to provide intelligent conversations and accurate medical advice to patients. The chatbot's advanced capabilities enable it to assist with booking appointments, symptom checking, and medication reminders, among other services, making healthcare more accessible and efficient for patients.
